## Reflections
# 1. Why do you think Focus Bear was created?
At the start it was created for personal useage. Founder(Jeremy) wanted to have product that wanted a tool that would force him to get his yoga done instead of scrolling instagram. And  than it growth to full product. 

# 2. What problem is Focus Bear solving?
It helps people with ADHA(or similar) to manage their day, stay focused and to remove discraction factor.

# 3. Why do you think this mission is important?
Because for such people it is hard to maintain their jobs, health, and mental well-being witout discraction

# 4. Do you personally relate to any of the challenges that Focus Bear aims to solve?
Yes, sometimes. Currently I'm working on a fulltime job, on different projects as the same time. And sometimes it is hard to stay focused.
